TUBEFU


A fan comic of one of my personal favorite series, "Beastar" which have an unfortunate ending to the series which left a bad taste in my mouth despite the high expectations it set in the early chapters. TUBEFU ("The Unofficial Beastar Expanded Fan Universe"), aims to expand further the concepts and ideas of the setting while working with the official ending. Which I hoped would improved upon my writing and world building skills since I have to worked with building up from where the series officially left while building and expanding on the existing concepts of the setting and themes.

Currently it's became the main focus point for me despite me having an original IP in the working for years, the impact of the original ending of this series has made me reorient my priorities on writing on this project, other than improving my skill and growing my audience, is born out my personal interest and love of the setting and themes of the series's theme and setting. Since it's a more brutal, realistic depiction of an anthromorphic animal society. Despite for some of it flaw remains one of the few fictional setting that spark my interest into a fictional setting and it's themes.
