A long time personal projects in the working, PIF, has been my long time personal project. Start off as a spin-off from a old project titled, "At the break of dawn", as a simple
background information to one of the faction has taken over the former as my focus project. As my interest in fantasy and medieval society and histroy became more deeper and in-depth.
Taking inspiration from medival europe, PIF takes place in an Imperial Society loosely based around 9th-16th century germany, specifically based on the HRE (Holy Roman Empire)
